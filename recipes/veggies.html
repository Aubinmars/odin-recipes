Veggies

Veggies is a delicious and nutritious dish made with a variety of fresh vegetables. It can be prepared by cooking the vegetables with seasonings and spices to create a flavorful and healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • Assorted vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, yellow squash, red onion)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Preheat a pan or skillet and add a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables to the pan and saut√© them until they are tender and lightly browned.
  4. Season the veggies with salt and pepper to taste while cooking.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and serve the deliciously cooked veggies.
