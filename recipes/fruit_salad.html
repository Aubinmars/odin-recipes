Fruit Salad

A refreshing and colorful mix of various fresh fruits, perfect as a dessert or snack.

Ingredients

  • Fresh assorted fruits of your choice
  • Optional: Lemon or lime juice
  • Optional: Honey or sugar
  • Optional: Fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. Wash and prepare the fruits.
  2. Cut larger fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Combine the fruits in a mixing bowl.
  4. Optional: Add a drizzle of lemon or lime juice for citrusy flavor.
  5. Optional: Add a touch of honey or sugar for added sweetness.
  6. Gently toss the fruits to mix them together.
  7. Optional: Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
